Our Mission

At our Community Watch Group, our mission is to create a safer neighborhood for everyone. We aim to foster a sense of community and cooperation among residents, working together to prevent crime and promote safety.

Community Safety Tip

Our Patrols

Take an active role in safeguarding our community by joining our neighborhood watch patrols! Sign up today, and receive patrol timetables conveniently delivered to your email. Together, we'll create a safer, more secure neighborhood we can all be proud to call home.

We Work Alongside Guards

We are proud to collaborate hand in hand with the Garda Síochána to enhance safety and security in our neighborhood. Through this strong partnership, we foster a united front against crime. Together, we strive to make a positive impact, creating a neighborhood where vigilance and cooperation prevail. Join us in this valuable alliance and be part of a safer, stronger community.
